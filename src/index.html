About Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird is a classic arcade game where you guide Faby, a small bird, through a series of obstacles by tapping or pressing the spacebar. Test your reflexes and aim for the highest score possible!

How to Play

  • Tap or press the spacebar to make Faby flap.

  • Avoid the pipes to stay alive.

  • Earn points by passing through each pipe set.

Use the Space key or Mouse to navigate
